Caibo Knitting Co., Ltd is an integrated knitting company based in China supplying global world class brands from USA, Europe and Asia with knitted products.  We are passionate about our textiles products, continuously pushing the frontiers the textiles world into the new world of sustainability and innovations.  Caibo Knitting aims to find new production methods and processes in our goal to be a part in the global mission to help save the world.

Our integrated line allows for this ability starting from designing our own pellets which we draw into our own yarn designs and then on to the knitting process that we may be able to design ourselves as well.  The ability to design our own pellets, yarns and even design our own knitting processes means that we will be able to go into areas where most suppliers may not prefer and show that it is possible, affordable and most importantly does not cause harm to our world.
