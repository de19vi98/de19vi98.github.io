MISION

The textile industry has been a dirty industry for far too long and it's time to clean up.  Many years ago the available technology to be clean and still survive in the textile industry was not yet feasible.  Now it is possible to be clean and flourish in our industry.  

Caibo Knitting aims to embrace sustainable solutions, adopt clean technologies and produce clean, yet affordable textiles for our world.  Higher technologies used means other than being clean our textiles will also have better quality as compared to textiles made using current conventional methods.

We do not seek to be the best because it is more important to be responsible to mother Earth. Our vision is to show that being sustainable and affordable is possible now with currently available technologies.


With a vision to embrace clean technologies Caibo Knitting mission is to show to the world that our products are affordable and better performing than our conventional counterparts.

Most clean green products that we have seen carry a heavy premium. Caibo Knitting products seeks to level this premium to a competitive level, with premium to be provide for performance and better new quality ideas.

Caibo Knitting now is at the gates to a brave new world and our mission is to go forth towards the horizon into areas where none or few textile manufacturers have gone before, make affordable products and still stay profitable. Along our innovative path we will pick up better know-how, bank on new experiences in our mission to help save the world through the use of sustainable responsible products.



Certification

Caibo Knitting are certified in ISO 9001:2015 as well as ISO 14001:2015.  


Products are regularly tested for Restricted Substances List, chemical testing done by Third Party testing institutions to ensure that Caibo products are free of Restricted Substances that may be harmful to people that wear/use our products as well as harmful to our environment.


We are also processing other certifications for Laboratory Accreditations, Sustainability Certifications and other certifications as well.
